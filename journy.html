1948

Set up as India's first Development Financial Institution

Milestone

At the time of independence in 1947, the Indian capital markets were relatively less developed. Against this backdrop and to bridge the demand-supply gap for capital needs of the economy, the Government of India established The Industrial Finance Corporation of India (IFCI) on 1st July 1948 by enacting the IFC Act, 1948. IFCI was the first Development Financial Institution of India set up to propel economic growth through development of infrastructure and industry.
