What We Are

Driving India’s Industrial Growth Through Financial Empowerment Since 1948

Institutional Identity

IFCI is a Systemically Important NBFC-ND-SI in the public sector established in 1948. Listed on BSE & NSE, IFCI operates with six subsidiaries and one associate company.

Diverse Financial Mandate

IFCI supports national industrial growth across sectors such as airports, highways, telecom, power, real estate, manufacturing and more.

Iconic projects supported include Adani Mundra Port, GMR Goa Airport, NRSS Transmission, Salasar Highways etc.

Government Advisory

IFCI is the Project Management Agency (PMA) for major Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes under the “Atmanirbhar Bharat” initiative and is the Verifying & Monitoring Agency for several capital subsidy programmes.

Corporate Advisory Services

IFCI provides financial advisory, ESG advisory, project advisory and handles monitoring of Sugar Development Fund (SDF) loans since 1984.

Institution Building

IFCI has played a crucial role in establishing stock exchanges, consultancy bodies, entrepreneurship & skill development institutions across India.

SC Entrepreneurship Initiatives

Govt. of India created a ₹200 Cr Venture Capital Fund for Scheduled Castes with IFCI as sponsor & lead investor.

IFCI Venture Capital Funds Ltd. manages this fund, supporting SC entrepreneurs.

Credit Enhancement Scheme

Since 2015, IFCI is the Nodal Agency for the Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for Scheduled Castes, helping SC entrepreneurs secure business loans.
